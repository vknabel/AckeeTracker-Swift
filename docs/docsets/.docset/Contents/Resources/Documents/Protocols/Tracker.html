Tracker

public protocol Tracker

The Ackee Tracker to be used across your app. Prefer using this protocol instead of the concrete AckeeTracker implementation.

  • record(_:)

    Creates a new record on the server and updates the record constantly to track the duration of the visit. The update of old records will be canceled when you call this function once again.

    Typically names of your ViewControllers will be provided here. Alternatively you could provide deep linking URLs to directly access contents from within your Ackee instance.

    ackee.record("MyController")
ackee.record("deep/linking/url")
